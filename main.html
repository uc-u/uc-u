ALS (Anime Last Stand)

Home About How to play ALS Game Summary

Anime Last Stand is a popular tower defense game on Roblox where players summon anime characters to defend against waves of enemies. You can upgrade and evolve your units to build powerful teams. Key modes include Story Mode, Infinite Mode, and Raids. There are also high-difficulty modes like Legend Stages and Boss Rush. Recent updates added Mythic & Celestial units, a revamped Battle Pass with 100 tiers, and performance fixes. The game has a strong community on Discord and a Trello for updates and guides. For a mix of anime action and strategy, Anime Last Stand delivers a fun, constantly updated experience.
